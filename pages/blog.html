5 Simple Tips for Sustainable Living

Posted on October 1, 2025 By Sarah Johnson Category: Sustainable Living

Living sustainably doesn't have to be complicated or expensive. In fact, some of the most impactful changes are also the simplest. Here are five easy ways to reduce your environmental footprint starting today:

  • Switch to reusable shopping bags and water bottles
  • Reduce food waste by planning meals and composting scraps
  • Choose energy-efficient LED light bulbs
  • Support local farmers and reduce transportation emissions
  • Unplug electronics when not in use to eliminate phantom energy

Small changes add up to make a big difference when we all participate.

How Our Community Solar Project is Powering 50 Homes

Posted on August 10, 2025 By David Kim Category: Environmental News

Six months after its completion, the EcoGreen Community Solar Project is already exceeding expectations. The installation, located on the roof of the community center, is now generating enough clean energy to power approximately 50 nearby homes.

This innovative project allows residents who can't install solar panels on their own properties (due to shading, renting, or other limitations) to benefit from solar energy. Participants purchase shares in the project and receive credits on their electricity bills proportional to their investment.

The project has not only reduced carbon emissions but also created local jobs and strengthened community ties. We're exploring possibilities for expanding the program to serve even more households.
