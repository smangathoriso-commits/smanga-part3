About Us

EcoGreen Solutions is a nonprofit organization dedicated to promoting sustainable living and environmental conservation through education, community engagement, and innovative solutions. Founded in 2015, we've grown from a small community initiative to a nationwide movement with over 50,000 active members.

Our Mission

To inspire and empower communities to adopt eco-friendly practices for a healthier planet through education, advocacy, and hands-on initiatives that make sustainability accessible to everyone.

Our Vision

We envision a world where sustainable living is the norm, not the exception - where communities thrive in harmony with nature, and future generations inherit a healthy, resilient planet.

Our Impact

Since our founding, EcoGreen Solutions has:

  • Organized over 500 community clean-up events removing 25+ tons of waste
  • Educated 15,000+ students through our school outreach programs
  • Helped 2,000+ households implement sustainable practices
  • Planted 10,000+ trees in urban and rural areas
  • Partnered with 50+ local businesses to reduce their environmental footprint
